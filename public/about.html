Our Mission

We are building the bridge between citizens and administrators to foster safer and cleaner communities through technology.

The Vision

Complainsy was founded on a simple principle: Civic engagement shouldn't be difficult. Often, residents see issues—potholes, broken streetlights, or illegal dumping—but the process to report them is opaque and frustrating. We saw an opportunity to utilize modern web technologies like accurate GPS tracking, secure cloud storage, and automated notification systems to fix this disconnect.

By providing a seamless digital platform, administrators can easily triage issues, map out high-problem zones, and dispatch resources efficiently. Meanwhile, the residents are kept in the loop automatically, establishing a new standard of trust in local communities.

Core Team Focus

Security First

User data, passwords, and GPS locations are strictly protected by modern AES encryption and JWT verification standard.

Speed & UI

Performance matters. The Complainsy system operates with lightning fast APIs and a 100% responsive, dark-mode focused UI.

Mapping Logic

We integrate with highly accurate map layers to drop precise pins, reducing search times for administrative fixers.
